A mental health initiative — for youth, by youth

Youth First-Aider Programme

A 2-Day workshop-esque programme involving 20 - 25 participants conducted in educatinal institutions, including high schools, colleges and universities that generates awareness and instils knowledge and skills to assist peers affected by mental health issue.
